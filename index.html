Pipeline Funnel

This funnel tracks how raw Google Maps leads are progressively filtered down to a workable outbound list. Each step removes accounts that don't meet quality or relevance thresholds.

  • Initial Accounts — Raw leads scraped from Google Maps via Serper across all target verticals in the US.
  • After Low Review Filter — Removes thin/unestablished businesses: keeps only accounts with more than 3 reviews.
  • After Keyword & Type Filters — Removes accounts whose business name or type doesn't match retail targets. Rescued accounts (from supplemental sources) are added back in here.
  • Kept as ICP — AI retail check: removes accounts flagged as non-ICP (wrong business type, franchise HQs, non-retail, etc.).
  • Contact Ready — Final working set after removing keyword-flagged accounts and place ID duplicates. These go to Clay for contact enrichment.
